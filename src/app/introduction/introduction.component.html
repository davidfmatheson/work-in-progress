Introduction

Lyrics remind me of you

I'll admit that I listen to a lot of music which is hard to decipher, but sometimes it grabs me because it makes me think about something in our relationship, and I thought I'd take our anniversary as motivation to build this site. I know you really like these, and I wish I had the creativity to do them more often, but it takes me months just to think this stuff up, and rummage around in my brain for the choice lines.

I pulled together six tracks from over the years, and I tried not to use any of the stuff I've used before. I can't figure out a way to do this besides Spotify, so you'll need that running when you look at this stuff. On the plus side, it's an honest-to-god website, so you can look at it whenever you want. I would say that Waving Through A Window was on the list way back when I conceived of this, but you added it to the kids' bedtime list, which is a definite killer when it comes to the enjoyment of a song.
